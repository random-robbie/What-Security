About Me

{% if site.tagline %}{{site.tagline}}{% else %}Security Researcher{% endif %}

I'm a UK-based security researcher known as "txt3rob" on HackerOne. I've made significant contributions to the cybersecurity community through extensive vulnerability research and reporting across various platforms.

Beyond HackerOne, I've demonstrated my expertise on platforms like Open Bug Bounty, where I've helped fix over 5,000 vulnerabilities. My approach involves mass scanning while ensuring no disruption to targeted websites, emphasizing my commitment to ethical hacking. I report vulnerabilities solely to inform and assist, without any expectation of compensation. More details about my work can be found on my Open Bug Bounty profile.

My contributions have earned me recognition in various Halls of Fame, including Dell, CERT-EU, CenturyLink, Auto Trader, AOL, and Apple. I actively participate in private bug bounty programs and am dedicated to sharing knowledge to strengthen the cybersecurity community.
